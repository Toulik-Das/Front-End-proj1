HTML <li> Tag



Example

One ordered (<ol>) and one unordered (<ul>) HTML list:

<ol>
  <li>Coffee</li>
  <li>Tea</li>
  <li>Milk</li>
</ol>

<ul>
  <li>Coffee</li>
  <li>Tea</li>
  <li>Milk</li>
</ul>

Definition and Usage

The <li> tag defines a list item.

The <li> tag is used in ordered lists, unordered lists , and in menu lists .
